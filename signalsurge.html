Signal Routing

Click neurons in the correct order to send a pulse down its real pathway before the signal decays to zero.

Watch for Decoys

Dashed, off-path nodes look tempting but aren't part of the circuit. Clicking one drains your signal — and teaches you why it's wrong.

Glowing orbs along the correct edges are neurotransmitters. Collect them to fill your charge meter and unlock bonuses.

Some pathways end in a live reflex test. Press SPACE (or tap the button) the instant the stimulus appears.

Brainstorm Mode

Certain pathways run two signals at once through different brain regions. Keep both alive — neither can hit zero.
